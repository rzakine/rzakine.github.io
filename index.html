Hello!
My name is Ruben Zakine and I am a second year PhD student in statistical physics.
I work at MSC laboratory, in Paris Diderot, under the supervision of Jean-Baptiste Fournier and Frédéric van Wijland .
I am interested in stochastic thermodynamics, active matter, and collective effects induced by deformation in soft matter systems.
Prior to this, I studied high energy physics at Ecole polytechnique and quantum mechanics at Ecole Normale Supérieure. In spring 2015, I also worked at Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris, under the supervision of Martin Lemoine.
