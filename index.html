Hello!
My name is Ruben Zakine and I am currently a post-doctoral associate at NYU MRSEC Research Center, working with Eric Vanden-Eijnden and Jasna Brujic.
I did my PhD at MSC laboratory, Paris Diderot, under the supervision of Jean-Baptiste Fournier and Frédéric van Wijland.
I work in the field of statistical physics and I am especially interested in active matter, nonequilibrium phase transitions, and collective effects induced by deformation in soft matter systems.
Prior to this, I studied high energy physics at Ecole polytechnique and quantum mechanics at Ecole Normale Supérieure. In spring 2015, I also worked at Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris, under the supervision of Martin Lemoine.
